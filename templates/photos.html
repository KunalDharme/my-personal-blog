    This website is a digital journal dedicated to cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and cyber forensic investigation. It documents real-world problems, cyber incidents, and suspicious digital activity that I personally investigate or analyze. Here, you’ll find detailed blog posts where I break down how I identify, trace, and understand the methods used by cyber attackers — including how I uncover hidden patterns, analyze digital evidence, and trace the origin of attacks using forensic tools and ethical hacking techniques.

    The content is crafted to raise awareness, educate readers, and demonstrate practical approaches to solving cyber threats — whether it's a phishing attempt, unauthorized access, social engineering, or data leaks. If you’ve been affected by a suspicious activity, you can reach out through the contact page to share your case. This platform is not just a showcase of findings, but a growing resource for anyone interested in understanding how cyber incidents are investigated and resolved responsibly, legally, and ethically.
